our hotel story

Our customers receive the best
We strive to always give the best service to our customers

Superior accomodation and warm personalized guest service

Kigala hotel is a convenient, quiet property located along Uhuru Road - one of the most iconic streets in the city.

The hotel is approximately 8km from Julius Nyerere International Airport, 1km from Dar es Salaam city centre, 4km from Ubungo bus terminal, 3km from Dar es Salaam Railway Station and just 3km from Zanzibar Ferry terminal on the Indian Ocean.

why choose our hotel

We have an abundance of services and a range of facilities

Constructed with 5 flours, the hotel comprises of 40 well furnished royal guest rooms and a top roof conference room. Each room contains an en-suite bathroom and a separate shower, split air condition units, in-house telephone service, a mini fridge and 32" flat screen with remote controlled satellite TV including both local and international DSTV channels.

  • Restaurant & Terrace Bar

    We serve our guests a wide selection of dishes from A la carte breakfast, lunch and dinner.

    Guests can occassionally be served buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner with a wide selection of fresh food items.

    The bar is stocked with local and imported beers, spirits, winess and a variety of soft drinks and bevarages.

  • Conference

    The top roof conference room has a sitting capacity of up to 50 people (depending on the sitting arrangement).

  • Laundry Service

    Laundry service is available during the day. We provide express laundry service without additional cost.

  • Transport

    We arrange pick up and transfers for clients arriving by airplane, train, boat or bust.

    The transport arrangement is available on request and on affordable transport charges.

  • Security

    The compound has 24 hours electronic securiy surveillance and security guards available.

  • Spacious parking

    There is an adequate spacious and secure parking for hotel residence customers.

  • City Tour

    We can arrange city tours for our hotel residents upon request.

  • High customer satisfaction

    Almost all our customer report to have had a positive and pleasant experience by staying with us.
